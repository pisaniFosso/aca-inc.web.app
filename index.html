About Us

We Are dedicated

We Cameroonians residing in the Canadian provinces of the Atlantic region (Prince Edward Island, New Brunswick, Nova Scotia and Newfoundland), convinced of the need to create among Cameroonians a climate of understanding and solidarity and mutual aid, anxious to constantly promote the image of Cameroon, We decide to create a non-profit, apolitical and secular association called Amicale des Camerounais de l'Atlantique, abbreviated A.C.A. Inc.
